Tell Me What To Do, Spinner Wheel!

Rules: Tired of making decisions of what to do next?
Complete the sentence after 'I will...' with your choices,
and the spinner wheel will choose one of them randomly three times.
Simply enter anything on each 6 different cone's text box and click SPIN!
RESET will reset only the sentence and restart the process.
Example: I will... 'drink beer' 'with a happy smile' 'after coding'
